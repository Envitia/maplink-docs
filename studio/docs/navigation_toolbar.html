Navigation Toolbar
Button Purpose
Zoom Mode
  • Single Shot Zoom: Click in the Map View to zoom in by a single step. Right-click in the Map View to zoom out by a single step. You can also use the Zoom In and Zoom Out buttons for single shot zooming.
  • Continuous Zoom: Click and hold in the Map View to zoom continuously in to the layer. Right-click and hold in the Map View to zoom out back to the original view of the layer.
  • Zoom to Rectangle: Create a rectangle by clicking and dragging the Map View, to zoom into the selected area.
Pan Mode
  • Pan To Point: Click in the Map View to pan to the selected location.
  • Continuous Pan: Click and hold in the Map View to enter continuous pan mode.
Feature Mode

Feature mode provides you with quick access to the rendering of selected features. Double-click on the feature in the Map View to open the Feature Book at the correct entry, or to move to the correct entry in the Feature Book if it is already open.

Zoom In

Zoom in to the Map View by a single step.
Zoom Out

Zoom out of the Map View by a single step.
Reset

Reset the Map View to show the full extent of the layer.
