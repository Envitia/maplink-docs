U.S. Census TIGER/Line

This is the support for the old format of TIGER/Line. The data is now supplied as Shapefile.

TIGER/Line files are a digital database of geographic features, such as roads, railroads, rivers, lakes, political boundaries, census statistical boundaries, etc. covering the entire United States.

Data can be obtained from here:

  • http://www.census.gov/geo/www/tiger/shp.html
